Unmasking YouTube Misinformation
Social media is in crisis. Every day, millions are exposed to misleading or outright false information online. Viral videos can shape opinions, fuel division, and even endanger lives. The rapid spread of misinformation on platforms like YouTube affects elections, public health, and our trust in each other.

FactorFiction helps you quickly check if a YouTube video is real or fake, empowering you to make informed decisions and fight back against the tide of digital deception.
